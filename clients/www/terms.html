MARGIN DISCLOSURE STATEMENT

This document includes basic facts about purchasing digital assets, derivative contracts, or legal tender on margin, and information about risks associated with trading with margin. Review this statement before trading any margin assets. Please contact support at [SUPPORT EMAIL] if you have any questions or concerns about margin trading.

When you place trades for futures contracts, or sell a prediction contract, you do not pay or receive cash when the trade takes place. Your cash flow may occur when you close out the position, or when the contract marks to market. In order to ensure that you will have enough cash to make all necessary payments, you will be required to keep a margin balance on hand. If your assets decline in value, your margin requirement may increase past your available cash, and as a result we will take action, such as issue a margin call and/or close out your positions, in order to maintain the required equity in your account.

It is important to fully understand the risks involved in trading with margin. These risks include:

  • You can lose more funds than you deposit . If the assets you hold decline in value you may be required to provide additional funds in order to avoid the forced sale of the assets in your account.
  • We can force the sale of assets in your account . If your account balance falls below our maintenance margin requirements, we will close your positions to cover the margin deficiency. These positions will be closed quickly and may not receive optimal pricing if the market is not liquid. You will be responsible for any shortfall that remains after your positions are closed out.
  • We can sell your assets without contacting you . We do not need to contact you for a margin call to be valid. Although we will make an effort to contact you in the event of a margin call, we are not required to. Therefore whether or not we have contacted you, we may close out your permissions without your consent.
  • We can increase margin requirements at any time and are not required to provide you with advance written notice . Markets are often volatile and sometimes require the immediate increase of margin requirements. Such an increase may result in an immediate margin call. Your failure to satisfy the call may result in the liquidation of your account. We are not responsible to delays in the release of funds intended to satisfy the call.
  • You are not entitled to an extension of time on a margin call . While we may sometimes offer an extension under certain circumstances, you do not have the right to one.
